# v10.5 Interactive Audit & Meeting Recap

## Interactive charts
- Animated bars, radar, donut and line drawing.
- Hover tooltips on audit charts.
- Clickable legend on the 30-day performance chart to show/hide Spend, Registrations and Rolling CPL.
- Click a day to pin its visual highlight.
- Timeline annotations appear as markers on the 30-day performance chart.

## Meeting recap & change impact
Each timeline annotation can be selected inside Audit overview. The dashboard compares 3, 7 or 14 days before and after the annotation, using daily-normalised metrics.

For creative changes it also separates ads first seen after the annotation from existing ads in the same post-change window. This makes it possible to review the contribution of newly published creatives.

The generated summary is explicitly observational and should not be treated as causal attribution because budget, audiences, seasonality and other concurrent changes may also affect results.
